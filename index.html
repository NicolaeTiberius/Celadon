About Us

We are the first company to use grass-cutting to the next level, powered with AI, it can cut other material apart from grass

This is a new generation of cutting-edge tools that assist with any materials

Have you ever wondered or been annoyed you cannot get a bunch of jobs done with the same equipment, sort of like an army knife but for raw materials such as glass, grass, wood etc.

With the power of AI, we have officialy developed a machine that first detects the material automatically and cuts to a set standard of your choice with the right blades and cleaning equipment provided.

We promise that unlike other machines, this wont let you down at all anytime anywhere.

  • Cuts safely for you without you having to move or touch it yourself.
  • Detects the right material and immediately responds.
  • Charged with 12 hours of cutting and fast re-charging.
